Personal information. DOB: January 1st, 1960. Address: 123 Elm Saint, Springfield, IL 62701. Personal background and prior business experience. Born in Chicago, IL, where he lived until attending college at Radford University in Radford, Virginia, where he earned a degree in accounting. He later earned an MBA and became a certified public accountant. After school, he worked at Compudyne Corp, a maker of security systems in Annapolis, Maryland. From August 1987 to March 1990, he was the chief financial officer of Quanta Systems Corp. He then moved to Quantronics, which was acquired in 1992 by Excel Technologies, a laser manufacturing company. In 1995, he joined the struggling Excel Technologies as chief executive officer. He became the CEO of Excel Technologies in 2000 and helped revive the company until it was bought by the software company Coherent for 376 million dollars in 2008. Bespoke Motor Group. After the sale of Excel Technologies, he stayed on as a consultant before receiving an offer to buy the ailing Champion Motor Group's Bentley store in 2010. Champion Motor Group then declared bankruptcy, lost its Lamborghini franchise, and sold its Rolls-Royce franchise to John, who founded Bespoke Motor Group. After a three-year hiatus, Bespoke Motor Group opened Lamborghini Long Island in 2013. Estimated annual revenues of 150 million dollars. Located at 456 Main Saint, Jericho, NY 120753. Notable stories and passion for automotives. John had always been an avid automotive enthusiast, owning cars such as a Ferrari F40, Ferrari F50, Ferrari Enzo, and Ford GT. John became one of only three handpicked customers to purchase the four-million-dollar Lamborghini Veneno in 2013. The purchase of the Veneno was completed before the car was even built, with only a rough artist rendering to show for it. He also owns one of only nine Lamborghini Veneno Roadsters, which was unveiled on December 1st, 2013.
